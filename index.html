About

Engineering outcomes,
not just dashboards.

I'm an AI Engineer and Data Scientist based in Porto Alegre, Brazil, with a trajectory that spans audit, big data, media intelligence, education, and now artificial intelligence. My career is defined by one pattern: I walk into complex organizations, find the leverage points, and build systems that produce measurable results.

At Yara, the world's largest fertilizer company, I automated reconciliation processes and recovered US$600,000 for my very first client. At Grupo RBS, Southern Brazil's largest media group, I coordinated 5 data squads and drove the largest Black Friday in company history. Then I founded Data Mundo, training over 6,000 professionals and placing 900+ of them at companies like Nubank, Shopee, and Uber.

Today, I build end-to-end AI ecosystems -- from WhatsApp agents and ML pricing engines to predictive demand models and managerial BI platforms. I speak four languages, write production code, and lead teams. I don't just analyze data. I build products that move businesses forward.
